DIAMOND PICKAXE

Material: Diamond  
Creator: Steve (Minecraft)  
Durability: Extremely high; endures harsh conditions and intense mining sessions  
Abilities: Fast mining speed; efficiently mines stone, diamonds, and emeralds  
Limitations: Cannot mine bedrock or other unminable blocks; requires enchantments for obsidian  
History: Crafted by Steve during extensive mining quests (e.g., "Project: Dig 100x100" and "Operation: Nether"); lost in the End during an escape from Endermen  
Crafting Recipe: 3 diamonds + 2 sticks  
Notable Events: First diamond mined; attempted mining of 16 obsidian blocks without proper enchantments; pickaxe lost in the End
