VEGAN MINK FUR COAT

Material: 100% synthetic (designed to mimic real mink fur)  
Design: High-end ethical fashion alternative offering luxury without animal cruelty  
Usage: Ideal for winter galas, climate protests, and upscale events; widely favored by influencers and environmental advocates  
Properties: Exceptionally soft texture and weather-resistant; projects an image of ethical luxury  
Limitations: Not embraced by traditional fur industry events; may be critiqued for its close resemblance to real fur  
History: Developed by a visionary designer; evolved from an initial potato fiber prototype; debuted at Vegan Fashion Week and quickly became a symbol of eco-friendly luxury
