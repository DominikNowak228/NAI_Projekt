PLEASE, JUST THREE – HOW TO PASS EVERY SUBJECT

Title: "PLEASE, JUST THREE – HOW TO PASS EVERY SUBJECT"  
Author: Dr. Max Chill  
Format: 32-page guide with 2 bonus coloring pages  
Purpose: Provides simple, minimal-effort exam tips to reduce stress and improve performance  
Features: Offers straightforward, practical advice; claims to reduce exam stress by 50%  
Limitations: May not satisfy professors who demand rigorous study methods  
History: Became a cult bestseller; infamously burned by the "Overachievers Club"; later adopted as mandatory reading at several universities
